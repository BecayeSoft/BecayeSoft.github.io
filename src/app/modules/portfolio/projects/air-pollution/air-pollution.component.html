Deaths caused by Air Pollution Arround the World

Context

Air pollution has caused thousands of deaths over the years. Kevin, Hanae and I decided to study those data to see how it impacted North and West Africa But the data we found on the Internet was not a the right format for our project. That's why we decided to perform ETL on them.

The following image shows what the original dataset looked like.

Entity Relationsip Diagram

We decided to transform each type of pollution into a hierarchy, and created 2 dimensions Country, Year and a table Pollution which served as a link between the Hierarchies and the dimensions. Finally, our table fact contained the measures and the KPI.

ETL

The ETL involved exttracting data form Excel files to create the hierachies, then we used SQL to get the hierachies ID and store them in the dimension Pollution. For the fact table, we used SQL to get all the measures and relevant IDs.

Results

In the end, we got these beautifully formatted tables ready for analysis and visualization.
